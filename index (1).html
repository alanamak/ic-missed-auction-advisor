Advanced assumptions

“Auction recovery” is applied dynamically to each campaign’s missed-auction opportunity, not as a fixed spend increase. For example, at 40% missed auctions, Good funds 25% of that missed opportunity, Better funds 50%, and Best funds 75%. Because the recommendation adds budget without increasing CPC bids, all three scenarios use the campaign’s full L7 ROAS to estimate attributed sales lift.

Recommendations

Dynamic model: Full recoverable daily spend opportunity = L7 average daily spend × missed auction % ÷ (1 − missed auction %). Good, Better, and Best then fund the selected share of each campaign’s own remaining opportunity. Target missed auction % = current missed auction % × (1 − recovery share). Estimated sales lift = incremental spend × the campaign’s L7 ROAS. The model assumes ROAS remains consistent because the recommendation increases available budget without changing CPC bids. These are directional planning estimates, not guaranteed delivery or sales.
